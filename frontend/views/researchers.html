Leading Paleontologists

Behind every groundbreaking discovery is a dedicated scientist. Learn about the legendary figures who unearthed the greatest prehistoric finds and shaped our understanding of the Mesozoic Era.

Barnum Brown

Known as **"Mr. Bones,"** he discovered the first documented skeletons of **T. rex** and a nearly complete *Corythosaurus*. His work defined the scope of modern field paleontology.

Key Discoveries: T. rex, Ankylosaurus

Jack Horner

Famous for his work on **parental care in dinosaurs** and the *Maiasaura* nesting grounds. He also served as a technical advisor for the popular ***Jurassic Park*** films.

Key Discoveries: Maiasaura, Dino Nests
